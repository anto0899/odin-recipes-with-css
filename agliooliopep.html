Let's cook some Spaghetti aglio olio e peperoncino!

Description

Spaghetti aglio, olio e peperoncino, often referred to as "spaghetti with garlic, oil, and chili pepper," is a classic Italian pasta dish known for its simplicity and bold flavors.
The dish originates from the Lazio region of Italy and is cherished for its quick preparation and delightful taste.

Ingredients

(4 servings)

  • 320 grams of spaghetti
  • 3 chili peppers
  • 3 garlic cloves
  • 70 grams of Olive oil
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Steps

  1. Cook the pasta
    Get some water to boil in a pot. When it starts boiling add some salt and throw the pasta in; cook for the indicated time on the package.
  2. Prepare the condiments
    In the meantime pour the oil in a pan and heat it up. Mince the garlic cloves and the chili peppers and fry them lightly (the so called "soffritto") being careful not to burn them.
  3. Stirring
    Drain the pasta and put it directly into the pan; Stir it briefly; if too dry, add some cooking water. Season with pepper to taste.
  4. Enjoy
